Welcome

New to coding?
Not sure where to start?
I was in the same boat just four months ago. I strongly credit fellow coders who share their insights via the internet and the use of AI for coding good, as the tools that helped me and will help you too. These lists are the sites that helped shape my knowledge and are being added to weekly. I only list sites that offer free services so PLEASE shout out if something has changed. I believe that learning to code does not need to drain your wallet but fair warning, it will drain your mind. Remember to take a break, drink some water and get some sunlight in between your projects.

I hope you find something useful here. If not, feel free to reach out and let me know what's missing.
