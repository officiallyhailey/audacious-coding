New to coding?
Not sure where to start?
I was in the same boat just four months ago. With support from the vast online coding community and AI as a learning tool, I've come so far and you can too. To showcases my abilities in both front and back end development, I decided to make a resource library comprised of the free services I learned from.
Learning to code does not need to drain your wallet but fair warning, it will drain your mind. Remember to take a break, drink some water and get some sunlight in between your commits.

I hope you find something useful here and if have suggestions or feedback, click the on the bottom of the page!
